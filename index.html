A Little More

Yes, we have competitors. We've seen them. Walking out of their car with a feather-duster and some all purpose spray.

We walk into a job with a team, a Sebo vacuum, spray bottles, multipurpose cleaner, 4-5 different types of Norwex rags, a Norwex mop (dry and wet), buckets, brushes, furniture polishes, stainless steel cleaner, scrub pads, and KNEE PADS! But, you will never see us with a duster. Dusters just move stuff around. Our goal walking in is to get all the dust into either the vacuum or the cleaning bucket and then getting it out of your house for good.
